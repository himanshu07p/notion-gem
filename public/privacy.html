Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 9, 2025

1. Information We Collect

The Gemini AI Assistant for Notion collects and processes:

  • Notion page content that you choose to analyze
  • Usage data for improving the service
  • Authentication tokens for Notion integration

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide AI analysis and content generation
  • Improve our AI models and services
  • Ensure secure access to your Notion workspace

3. Data Protection

Your data is protected through:

  • Encrypted transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure API integration with Notion and Google Gemini
  • No permanent storage of your content

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with:

  • Notion API: To read and write content in your workspace
  • Google Gemini API: To provide AI analysis and generation
